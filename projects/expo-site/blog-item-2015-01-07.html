Come to see us at ArabPlast 2015 (10-13 Jan) in Dubai
07.01.2015

ExpoPlatform LTD an event web platform and mobile app provider supports ArabPlast 2015 in Dubai.

If you are an organiser of exhibitions/conferences in the MENA region you are most welcome to visit ArabPlast, meet our team there and discuss the possibility of cooperation on your events. ArabPlast is the leading and LARGEST plastics, petrochemicals and rubber industry trade show in the MENA region.

From 10th - 13th January we will be supporting ArabPlast 2015 which takes place at Dubai World Trade Centre and has about 1000 exhibitors and over 25 000 visitors, with our interactive web portal and mobile app.

Come and see us on stand number 8Z146 at the exhibition enterance, where we'll be on hand to answer any questions visitors and exhibitors may have about our web platform and mobile application features, and to discuss with them how their companies will benefit from the business networking our technology enables.
