Fan Made Video

January 21, 2014

Taking a product to market comes with a lot of burden. Beyond the time and money spent, is the pride, ego, or self-esteem that’s exposed. It’s a huge blow when someone dislikes what you’ve put everything, including hopes and dreams, into.

However, on the flip side, is when someone (especially your target audience) loves what you’ve done. When they send in a video that they personally created – it’s awesome! Electrifying!

We are extremely humbled when young boy (and his mother too) takes time to create and share their thoughts about our product. So without further ado, here is a fan-created video from Mikey - an 8 year old from Oregon.

Buddy, you make what we do sooo satisfying…thank you.
