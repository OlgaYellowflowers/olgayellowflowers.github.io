Verification Stage Description:
Verification Stage indicates the progress on borrower Prosper loan application, based on Prosper's verification of information and documents borrower have submitted.
Verification Stage 1 - The borrower needs to submit documentation for Prosper to verify.
Verification Stage 2 - Prosper has verified most of the information provided by the borrower.
Verification Stage 3 - Prosper has verified most of the information provided by the borrower. Additional documentation may still be required, but loans in Verification Stage 3 are very likely to originate.
