STAGE: MATURE SAAS BUSINESS

MAINTAINING OPERATIONAL EXCELLENCE WHILE CONTINUING TO SCALE

is the major challenge of the successful mature SaaS business. Growing quickly puts a strain on operations, technology, and the business as a whole. This can lead to small breakdowns that increase costs, decrease eﬃciency, impact user experience and result in churn. At this stage, you’ll want to look at new ways to sell, review your technology platform to see if it will scale with you, and re-examine your operational and business processes and metrics.

Focus on:

SALES GROWTH

Investigate new regions, channels and think aggressively about upsell and cross-sell strategies to expand your sales footprint. Tightly align customer success with sales; a focus on customer experience is equally important to growing sales at this stage.

GO-TO-MARKET CAMPAIGNS
CHANNEL STRATEGY
BENCHMARKING
FINANCIAL MODELING
ECOSYSTEM DEVELOPMENT & PARTNERSHIPS

OPERATIONAL EFFICIENCY

Focus on the lowest levels of process efficiency and squeeze as much out of everything that you do as possible. It’s time to review and reﬁne your processes to automate scaling, organization and infrastructure to allow your business to grow in chunks.

RECOMMENDED SERVICES:
OPERATIONAL ASSESSMENT AND BENCHMARKING
FINANCIAL MODELING
ACTION PLANNING

SAAS METRICS

Continuously tune sales and marketing eﬀectiveness, maintaining high customer satisfaction levels, and product and process innovation through increasing granularity and variety of metrics. This will drive revenue growth and ultimately result in creating a high value SaaS company.
