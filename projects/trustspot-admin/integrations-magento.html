Magento Integration

Automated your review collection process with your Magento store by installing our Shopify Extension. The entire process takes less than a minute, and than you'll be collecting in no time! Have a Question? Check out our Magento Integration Guide .

Setup

Step 1: Install Magento Extension
To get started click on the "Install TrustSpot Extension" button below to be sent to the Magento Connect Store.

Install TrustSpot Extension

Step 2: Finding the extension in your Store
After you've successfully installed the extension into your store, you will need to connect your TrustSpot account by updating the extension settings.

You will find our extension installed within your Magento Admin panel, go to System > Configuration.

Click on the "TrustSpot Settings" tab on the left sidebar.

Step 3: API Credentials
Once you've opened the TrustSpot Settings tab, you will need to enter in your API Credentials so TrustSpot can connect to your store.

Step 4: Order Settings
By default orders that are marked with the status "Invoiced" will be sent to TrustSpot to start the review collection process. You can change this setting by selecting a different option from the drop down menu.
