Invite Customers

The invite customers feature allows you to send review requests directly to your customers from an imported CSV file. Simply upload your customer list, customize the content of your email and click Send Email.

ECUSTOMERS

Download Example CSV

TIP - Your customer csv file should only contain 3 columns: Customer Name, Email Address, Date of Purchase. Maximum of 1,000 rows (email addresses) per invite request.
