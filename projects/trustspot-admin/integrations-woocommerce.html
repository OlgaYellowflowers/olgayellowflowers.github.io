WooCommerce Integration

Automated your review collection process with your WooCommerce store by installing our WooCommerce Plugin. The entire process takes less than a minute, and than you'll be collecting in no time! Have a Question? Check out our WooCommerce Integration Guide .

Setup

Step 1: Download the WooCommerce Plugin
To get started click on the "TrustSpot Plugin" button below to download our plugin to your computer.

TrustSpot Plugin

Step 2: Install the WooCommerce Plugin
After you've successfully downloaded our plugin, you will need to install it to your Wordpress Site.

In your WordPress admin site, click on the "Plugins > Add New".
Click on "Upload"
Click on "Choose file". Then find the TrustSpot plugin zip file that you downloaded. Press "Install Now" and wait for the upload to complete.
As soon as the installation completes press on the "Activate Plugin" link.

Step 3: Account Login
Once you click Activate Plugin you will be brought to our Plugin Screen where you can login to your TrustSpot account.

Step 4: Order Settings
By default orders that are marked with the status "Invoiced" will be sent to TrustSpot to start the review collection process. You can change this setting by selecting a different option from the drop down menu.
